Brakes, Suspensions,
& Vehicle Tune-ups

Stay safe on the road with brake repair and vehicle tune-ups.
We can also add some style to your truck with suspension lifts and repairs.

Brake Repair

At Perry's Tire Service we handle all of your brake needs. From pads and rotors to brake lines and hoses, we use Napa parts (and our brake pads come with a lifetime warranty). Leave our repair bays with a quieter and safer car, thanks to our high-quality, semi-metallic brake pads.

Suspension Repair & Lifts

Keep your suspension parts, such as ball joints, tie rod ends, struts, and shocks, in perfect repair with our suspension services. Suspension parts wear out like everything else, so for the sake of your safety and vehicle handling, let us check your suspension today.

Truck Lifts

When you want your truck lifted, we install 2"–5" lifts. As a Rough Country Suspension Systems® dealer, we can turn your truck into an off-roading machine. We also install hitches and running boards for a finished look.

Tune-Ups & General Maintenance

Allow us to handle all of your general vehicle maintenance needs. We offer tune-ups and oil changes, and we also repair and replace heating and cooling systems, starters, and batteries. You should always follow your manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedule and modify it to suit your specific driving needs.
